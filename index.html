Introducing devDesk

Relax, devDesk has arrived and has your back!

Take Control of the Problems Around You!

With devDesk, issue submission forms are streamlined to get the maximum amount of information from the end user without overwhelming them. Clearly defined sections and labels make sure the end user gets you the information you need to solve any problem imaginable!

Don't let issues slip through the cracks!

With email and slack alerts, you will never forget an open ticket. Simple notifcations keep you abreast of any changes to the ticket in real time. Mark a ticket with three different levels of severity to make things easy to sort and view from above.

End User Notifcations keep everyone happy!

Once a ticket is checked out by you, the end user is automatically notified that their problems will soon be at an end! Cut out the middle man of sending an email to let the user know you are on their problem with notifcations on their slack or email with a click of a button.
