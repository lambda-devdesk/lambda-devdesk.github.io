About our mission.

Ticket solutions redefined!

A dream coded from nightmares!

The designers of devDesk have worked with countless ticketing programs before even hearing of Lambda School. Late nights at previous jobs fighting with these programs and problems gave us a clear path, a streamlined system designed to work with you rather than against you.

Our team started devDesk in the early summer days of 2019. With a small team of Lambda hardened coders, we pushed through each wall in front of us and made something we can be proud of, something truly special, and we think you will think so too.

By Lambda students, for Lambda students!

The best part of devDesk to us is the love that goes into the lines of code. Every line has been written by someone that walked the same path as those that will use it. We all share in the experence of Lambda School, making it simple to spot the pitfalls and fill them up with features that counter every issue we personally encountered in our time at Lambda. When you are using devDesk, you are standing on the top of the same mountain countless others have climbed!
